Install Server:

In order to use the app you will first have to download the server for your pc at: www.bit.ly/keyboardextender

Usage:

Press any button in the app to input the character(s) it represents without the need to type a shortcut on your keyboard.

Hold for about a second to access secondary functions on some buttons e.g. sin, cos, and tan.

Swipe left or right to access another keyboard layout.

In the settings menu you can choose which keyboard layouts are available to swipe to.

Creating a keyboard layout:

To create your own keyboard layout press the menu button located in the top right corner or built in to your smartphone.
Press "Edit layout"�

Press any keyboard button to edit its appearance and output.
Change the name of your layout by pressing the top blue bar.
To change the amount of buttons on your layout press the menu button then press "Edit size"� and choose the number of colons and rows.

Save your layout by pressing the menu button then "Save"�. Make a file name and press "OK"
Your layout(s) will be located under → Settings → Active layouts
